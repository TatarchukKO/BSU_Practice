NIKE FLYKNIT RACER “TRIPLE WHITE” RELEASES NEXT WEEK

The Nike Flyknit Racer is finally getting the Triple White treatment, and for a good cause too. The Swoosh is tapping into their Greek mythology background once again, this time paying homage to the goddess of victory with the Nike Flyknit Racer Goddess set to drop on March 8th in honor of International Women’s Day. And guys, don’t worry – this Triple White Flyknit Racer should be releasing in a full size run. This isn’t a traditional Triple White option either, as the midsole gets an off-white Sail treatment while you won’t have to worry too much about keeping the outsole clean thanks to the standard black peeking through. Check out more detailed shots below and expect this pair to drop on Nike.com next week.
